Scheduler

Instructions

Finding a Good Schedule

You will use the buttons below to calculate schedules.

  1. Choose your milestone setting. Milestones are used to discard unpromising schedules early in the search. The recommended option is the first button with the 10th percentile, which is more leniant.
  2. Choose how long the search will run. You can safely halve the time estimates given in parentheses at the end if you chose to use milestones. A light search might be 1000 iterations with 10000 steps, which will run in about a minute. A heavy search might be 100000 iterations with 10000 steps, which will run in about three hours.
  3. Right-click to open Inspect Element and switch to the "console" tab to see progress.

Viewing the Good Schedule

  1. A JSON string will be automatically copied into the text-box below when the search for a schedule is complete. If you wish to view a different schedule, copy its JSON string into the box instead.
  2. Click the "load JSON" button. A selection of teachers and specific independence levels will appear.
  3. Choose a category to load, or press "Load All Levels" to load them all. A selection of persons in the category will appear.
  4. Choose the person whose schedule you desire displayed and press "Load Schedule", or press "Load All Schedules" to load them all.
  5. Mouse over or touch blocks with a red bar for additional information about the conflict.
